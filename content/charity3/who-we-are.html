Transforming Jasawa Through Unity and Development

The Jasawa Community Development Association (JCDA) is a visionary initiative dedicated to peace-building and sustainable development in Jasawa.

Born from the urgent need to address complex socio-economic challenges and security issues, JCDA stands as a beacon of hope and progress.

Our comprehensive approach aims to restore peace, stimulate economic growth, and empower every citizen to contribute to a thriving, united community. Our Mission: Peace, Prosperity, and Progress

At JCDA, we envision a peaceful Jasawa where prosperity and opportunities abound, and every citizen is empowered to contribute to a thriving and united Nigeria.

Our mission is to:

1. Foster sustainable peace through dialogue and reconciliation

2. Drive economic growth and create job opportunities

3. Enhance education and skills development programs

4. Strengthen community bonds and cultural heritage

5. Promote environmental sustainability and resource management

Driving Change Through Action

Our key initiatives focus on:

  • Peace Building: Facilitating dialogue and reconciliation to address root causes of conflict.
  • Economic Empowerment: Creating job opportunities and supporting local businesses to boost the economy.
  • Education & Skills: Improving access to quality education and vocational training programs.
  • Community Development: Enhancing infrastructure and promoting cultural preservation.
