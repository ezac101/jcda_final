About JCDA

The Jasawa Community Development Association (JCDA) is dedicated to fostering peace, stability, and sustainable development in Jos and its environs.

Through our innovative JASAWA Action Plan, we address complex socio-economic challenges and security issues facing our community. Our comprehensive five-year peace-building and development initiative aims to restore peace and stimulate growth through a multi-stakeholder approach.

Our vision is to create a peaceful JCDA, where prosperity and opportunities abound, and every citizen is empowered to contribute to a thriving and united Nigeria.
